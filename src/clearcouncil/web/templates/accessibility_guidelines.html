Best Practices for ClearCouncil

Navigation & Structure
  • Provide skip links to main content
  • Use proper heading hierarchy (h1, h2, h3...)
  • Ensure keyboard navigation works everywhere
  • Add breadcrumb navigation
Content & Media
  • Alt text for all images and charts
  • Captions for videos
  • Transcripts for audio content
  • Plain language descriptions
Forms & Interaction
  • Label all form inputs clearly
  • Provide error messages and help text
  • Group related form fields
  • Ensure sufficient click targets
Visual Design
  • Maintain 4.5:1 color contrast ratio
  • Don't rely solely on color for meaning
  • Ensure text is resizable to 200%
  • Provide visible focus indicators
